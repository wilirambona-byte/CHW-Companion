CHW Companion

AI-Powered Support for Community Health Workers

CHW Companion is a WhatsApp-based conversational AI agent designed to strengthen the capacity of Community Health Workers during home visits and patient referrals in low-resource settings.

Background

AI and Primary Healthcare

Community health workers are the backbone of primary healthcare in low-resource settings, often serving as the first and only point of contact between patients and the health system.

Yet CHWs operate with limited training, scarce diagnostic tools, and no easy access to clinical guidance in the field. Recent research shows that large language models can respond to real CHW clinical questions with high accuracy, at a fraction of the cost of human expert consultation.

Capabilities

What the CHW Companion Is and Is Not

A CHW sends a message describing a patient's symptoms. The Companion replies with clear, evidence-based guidance, helping identify likely conditions, advise on referrals, and support documentation of the visit.

The Companion IS

  • ✔ A clinical decision support tool for CHWs
  • ✔ A real-time companion during home visits
  • ✔ A charting and documentation assistant
  • ✔ Designed for low-connectivity environments
  • ✔ A tool that supports referral pathways

The Companion IS NOT

  • ✘ A replacement for the CHW's own judgment
  • ✘ A diagnostic system or medical device
  • ✘ A substitute for a doctor or nurse
  • ✘ A telemedicine platform for patients
  • ✘ A standalone solution without oversight

Process

How It Works

A simple three-step interaction loop that fits the CHW's everyday routine, with no new apps and no special hardware required.

01
💬

CHW Sends a Message

The health worker describes a patient's symptoms or situation via WhatsApp, as they would reach out to a trusted colleague.

02
🧠

AI Analyses and Responds

The Companion processes the message and returns evidence-based guidance on conditions and referral status.

03
📋

CHW Documents the Visit

The interaction supports logging and record-keeping, with data remaining under national health authority control.

Governance

Privacy by Design

Patient data is sensitive. The CHW Companion is built with privacy protection at its core, woven into the design from day one.

🔒

No Identifiable Patient Data

CHWs describe symptoms without names or ID numbers. No identifiable data is stored by the AI system.

🤝

Community-Level Consent

Consent is sought from local leaders, recognising that community consent carries significant weight.

🏛️

Data Sovereignty

Health trends and usage patterns belong to the community and national health system.

🔐

End-to-End Encryption

Conversations are encrypted in transit. No content is used for training without explicit authorisation.
